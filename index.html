  • fits nicely in pocket

The drawbacks (smaller screen, smaller battery) are fundamentally less of an issue than the size, to me at least.

Why write this? I was listening to the Google announcement of the Pixel 7 last week and realized no one is never going to make another small Android phone unless we band together and show everyone how many people want one!
